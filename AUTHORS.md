# Authors and Contributors

This file recognizes the humans who have contributed to the Open Knowledge Pack Format project.

---

## Founder

**Charles Toepfer** — *Project Founder and Initiator*

Charles initiated OKPF as an open effort to develop a portable, vendor-neutral standard for packaging structured human expertise for AI systems and future computational tools. He designed the initial specification, schema architecture, reference implementations, and governance model, and has worked to ensure the project is open in governance and independent of any single organization or platform.

## Contributors

Contributors are recognized here as the community grows. To add yourself after a merged contribution, open a pull request adding your name and a brief description of your contribution.

*This list will grow as the project grows. If you have contributed and are not listed here, please open a pull request.*

---

## Recognition Format

When adding an entry, use this format:

```
**Your Name** — *optional title or affiliation*
Brief description of contribution (e.g., "Added support for X", "Authored example pack for Y domain").
```

Human expertise drives OKPF. The people who build and improve this format deserve clear, durable credit for their work.
