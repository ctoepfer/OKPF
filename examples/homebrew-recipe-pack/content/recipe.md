# Summit Pale Ale

**Style:** American Pale Ale  
**Batch size:** 5 US gallons (18.9 L)  
**Original gravity (OG):** 1.052  
**Final gravity (FG):** 1.012  
**ABV:** ~5.2%  
**IBU:** 38  
**SRM:** 6 (golden-amber)  
**Boil duration:** 60 minutes

---

## Grain Bill

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| 2-Row Pale Malt (American) | 9.5 lb (4.3 kg) | Base malt; provides fermentables and body |
| Crystal 40L | 0.75 lb (340 g) | Adds caramel sweetness and amber color |
| Victory Malt (or Biscuit Malt) | 0.25 lb (113 g) | Toasty, bread-like character |

**Total grain:** 10.5 lb (4.76 kg)  
**Expected mash efficiency:** 72%

---

## Hop Schedule

| Hop | Amount | Alpha Acid | Addition Time | Purpose |
|-----|--------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Cascade | 1.0 oz (28 g) | 5.5% AA | 60 min | Bittering |
| Centennial | 0.5 oz (14 g) | 10.0% AA | 15 min | Flavor |
| Cascade | 0.5 oz (14 g) | 5.5% AA | 5 min | Aroma |
| Cascade | 0.5 oz (14 g) | 5.5% AA | Dry hop (4 days) | Aroma |

---

## Yeast

**Strain:** Safale US-05 (dry) or equivalent American Ale strain  
**Attenuation:** ~75%  
**Fermentation temperature:** 65–68°F (18–20°C)  
**Pitch rate:** 1 packet (11.5 g) for 5 gallons at 1.052

---

## Water Profile Target

| Ion | Target (ppm) |
|-----|-------------|
| Calcium (Ca²⁺) | 75 |
| Magnesium (Mg²⁺) | 10 |
| Sodium (Na⁺) | 20 |
| Sulfate (SO₄²⁻) | 100 |
| Chloride (Cl⁻) | 60 |
| Bicarbonate (HCO₃⁻) | 50 |

This profile emphasizes sulfate over chloride to accentuate hop bitterness and crispness.

## Fermentation and Conditioning

1. Cool wort to 65°F (18°C) before pitching yeast.
2. Ferment at 65–68°F for 7–10 days until FG is stable.
3. Add dry hops at day 4–5 and leave for 4 days.
4. Cold crash at 35°F (2°C) for 2 days before packaging.
5. Carbonate to 2.4 volumes CO₂ (approximately 4.8 oz / 136 g corn sugar for bottle conditioning, or carbonate to ~12 PSI at 38°F if kegging).

---

## Brewer's Notes

This is a clean, citrus-forward American Pale Ale that showcases Cascade hops. The small additions of Crystal 40L and Victory malt add body and a toasty background without overpowering the hop character. The sulfate-forward water profile keeps the bitterness crisp and dry.

Variations:
- **Hop-forward version:** Increase the 5-min Cascade addition to 1.0 oz and add a 0.5 oz Centennial dry hop alongside the Cascade.
- **Maltier version:** Replace Victory malt with 0.5 lb Munich malt and reduce Crystal 40L to 0.5 lb.
- **Lower ABV:** Reduce 2-row to 8 lb for OG ~1.044, ABV ~4.2%.
