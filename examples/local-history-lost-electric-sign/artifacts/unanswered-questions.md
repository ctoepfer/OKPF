# Unanswered Questions

These questions are not answered by the source article transcribed in this
pack. They are preserved here as open research questions, not as claims
awaiting confirmation of a predetermined answer.

- Was the sign installed?
- Where exactly was it mounted?
- Was it placed diagonally, as discussed in the article, or in another
  orientation?
- Who owned or maintained it after installation?
- Did the Chamber of Commerce pay the estimated electricity costs after
  installation, and for how long?
- Are there photographs, postcards, city records, or permit records that
  document the sign?
- When was it removed?
- Was it damaged, relocated, replaced, or simply forgotten?

See [`records/research-leads.jsonl`](../records/research-leads.jsonl) for
candidate places a future researcher could look to answer these, and
[`records/negative-searches.jsonl`](../records/negative-searches.jsonl) for
how this pack records searches that have (or have not yet) been performed.
