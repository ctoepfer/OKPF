## Purpose

This SOP defines the standard process for initiating, recording, and following up on vendor contacts.

## Scope

Applies to any engagement with external vendors, suppliers, or service providers.

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Operations Coordinator | Primary point of contact for vendor inquiries |
| Treasurer | Approves purchases above budget threshold |
| Board Secretary | Archives signed vendor agreements |

## Initial Contact

1. Identify the need and confirm it is within budget before contacting a vendor.
2. Use the organization's general inquiry email address for all initial outreach.
3. Do not share personal contact information of board members with vendors without board approval.

## Vendor Evaluation

For new vendors providing services above the threshold amount:

1. Obtain at least two quotes.
2. Document the evaluation criteria used.
3. Present the recommendation to the board for approval.

## Record Keeping

All vendor contacts are logged in the organization's contact register with:

- Vendor name (placeholder: "Acme Supplies Co.")
- Date of contact
- Purpose of contact
- Outcome or follow-up required
- Name of staff member who made contact

## Escalation

Any vendor dispute or contract concern is escalated to the board chair and treasurer within two business days.
